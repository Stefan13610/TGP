\subsection{Summary of principal results}%
\label{subsec:nbody-results-summary}

The $N$-body analysis yields \textbf{eight} principal results,
each verified by dedicated regression tests (59/59 PASS,
102 active scripts in the \texttt{nbody/examples/} directory).

\begin{center}\small
\begin{tabular}{@{}clp{8.5cm}@{}}
\toprule
\textbf{\#} & \textbf{Result} & \textbf{Key finding} \\
\midrule
1 & Earnshaw violation
  & Equilateral 3-body configurations admit stable static equilibria
    with all normal modes $\omega^2 > 0$; Newton gives marginal modes. \\[4pt]
2 & Chaos suppression
  & TGP suppresses 3-body chaos by 35--45\% (maximal Lyapunov exponent)
    for $\beta \gtrsim 0.025$; enhanced by $V_3$. \\[4pt]
3 & $C_{\rm eff}$ from EFT
  & Yukawa source coupling derived from EFT projection of
    classical topological defect profile. \\[4pt]
4 & Classical $\neq$ Yukawa
  & Classical soliton interaction is oscillatory with power-law decay;
    Yukawa law requires the EFT mass gap. \\[4pt]
5 & $I_Y$ multipole form
  & Irreducible three-body Feynman integral admits semi-analytic
    multipole representation ($< 0.01\%$ at $L_{\max} = 15$). \\[4pt]
6 & $V_3/V_2$ regime map
  & Perturbative ($\sim 1\%$) in Lyapunov regime; exponential
    suppression for $m_{\rm sp}\,d > 3$. \\[4pt]
7 & Analytical equilibria
  & Closed-form equilibrium distances $d_{\rm rep}$, $d_{\rm well}$;
    radial normal-mode frequency; TGP Hill sphere generically expanded
    relative to Newton. \\[4pt]
8 & Unequal-mass phase diagram
  & Phase boundary $\beta_{\rm crit} = \frac{3}{2}\sqrt{\gamma(C_1+C_2)}$;
    escape velocity $v_{\rm esc} = \sqrt{2|V_2(d_{\rm well})|/\mu}$;
    breathing mode $\omega_{\rm br}^2 = \frac{3}{2}\,\omega_{\rm rad}^2$
    (exact), $V_3$ correction $-1.3\%$. \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\paragraph{Relation to main manuscript.}
The pairwise potential $V_2$ (gradient + repulsion + confinement)
corresponds to the three regimes of
Section~\ref{sec:rezimy}\footnote{If the label differs in
the actual manuscript, adjust accordingly.}.
The three-body force $V_3$ arises from the $\Phi^4$
self-interaction term in the TGP action
(Section~\ref{sec:formalizm}\footnotemark[\value{footnote}]).
The Yukawa EFT coupling $C_{\rm eff}$ provides the microscopic
origin of the source strength $C_i$ used throughout the
$N$-body analysis, connecting to the soliton mass hierarchy
of Section~\ref{sec:stale}\footnotemark[\value{footnote}].
