→ 4-of-4 compatible). Pełna UV-complete renormalizability wymaga:

  prove that gravitational FRG flow has UV non-Gaussian fixed point z finite-dimensional
  critical surface. Status: STRUCTURAL OPEN, multi-decade research community.

  one that gives TGP S_TGP jako massless mode. Status: anthropic / dynamical selection
  STRUCTURAL OPEN; KKLT (Kachru-Kallosh-Linde-Trivedi 2003) jest candidate, niepełny.

  has a well-defined continuum limit + 4D extended phase is in the right universality
  class. Status: Loll 2019 review OPEN; numerical evidence strong, analytical proof missing.
