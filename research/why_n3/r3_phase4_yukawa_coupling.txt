  SEKCJA 2: Yukawa coupling y = ∂m_eff/∂ψ — wyprowadzenie
==============================================================================

m_eff(ψ) = c_M · A_tail²(g₀(ψ)) · g₀(ψ)^(e²/2)

Logarithmic derivative:
  ln(m_eff) = ln(c_M) + 2·ln(A_tail(g₀)) + (e²/2)·ln(g₀)

  d(ln m_eff)/dψ = 2·(dA_tail/dg₀)/A_tail · dg₀/dψ + (e²/2)/g₀ · dg₀/dψ

  dg₀/dψ = 1/0.3814 = 2.622  (z Faza 1)

Yukawa coupling (logarithmic):
  y_log = d(ln m_eff)/dψ |_{ψ=ψ_gen}

  gdzie:
    m_0 = m_eff(1) — bare mass (DLA SOLITONU vacuum-stable JEST 0)
    y   = ∂m_eff/∂ψ |_{ψ=1}

  ψ_vacuum = 1.000

  >> m_0 ≈ 0 (vacuum mass znika) — emergent fermion ma zero bare mass
     w vacuum, all mass comes from displacement (ψ - 1).

==============================================================================
  SEKCJA 5: Path integral fragment dla solitonu
==============================================================================

Partition function (Euclidean) dla solitonu jako bound state:

  Z_soliton = ∫ Dψ Dψ̄ exp(-S[ψ̄, ψ; soliton bg])

Effective action dla spinora w soliton background:
  S = ∫ d⁴x ψ̄ [iγ^μ ∂_μ - m_eff(ψ_loc)] ψ
    = S_kin + ∫ d⁴x m_eff(ψ_loc) ψ̄ψ

Splitting wokół vacuum:
  ψ_loc = 1 + δψ_loc
  m_eff(ψ_loc) = m_0 + y · δψ_loc + O(δψ²)

  S = S_kin + m_0 ∫ ψ̄ψ + y ∫ δψ_loc · ψ̄ψ + O(δψ² ψ̄ψ)

  --> Drugi term: STANDARD Yukawa coupling y · δψ · ψ̄ψ
      w action. Coupling y wyznaczony powyzej dla każdej generacji.
