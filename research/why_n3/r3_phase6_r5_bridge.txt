==============================================================================
  SEKCJA 3: Soliton energy integral w R⁵
==============================================================================

  Hipoteza: w R⁵ z metryka ds² = -dt² + dr² + r² dΩ₃² (4D) + dψ²,
  soliton ma profil g(r, ψ). W limicie ψ-niezależnym, sektor R⁵ generuje
  factor wave-function renormalization Z(α):

    Z(α) = ∫ Dψ exp(- S_R⁵[ψ-fluctuations])

  Dla Gaussian fluctuations w ψ-direction wokół vacuum, naive:

    Z(α) ~ exp[(...)/(2π)^{5/2} · log(g₀)]

  Sprawdzmy czy ten exponent może dać e²/4·(4-α).

  RG flow scalar pole w 5D:
    β-function dla mass: β_m² = γ · m²
    γ = 2 - d (anomalous dim. mass dla scalar w D dim)
    Dla D=5: γ = 2 - 5 = -3 (ujemny anomalous dim, run to UV)

  Wave-function ren. Z(α) z RG:
    Z = exp(γ_φ · ln(μ/μ₀))

  Jeśli γ_φ ~ e²/4 i log(μ/μ₀) ~ ln(g₀)·(4-α)/4, dostajemy:
    Z = exp(e²/4 · (4-α)/4 · ln(g₀)) = g₀^{e²/4·(4-α)/4}

  ALE potrzebujemy g₀^{e²·(1-α/4)} = g₀^{(e²/4)·(4-α)}
  Diff: factor 4 mismatch (z (4-α)/4 do (4-α)).

  Możliwe wyjaśnienie: 4 = liczba spatial dim R⁴, daje ekstra factor.
