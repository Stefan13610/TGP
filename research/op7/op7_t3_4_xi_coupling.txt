  Q_ij = mu_red * x_i x_j  (kwadrupol momentum)
  mu_red = M_1 M_2 / (M_1 + M_2)

Druga pochodna czasowa:
  Q_ij_ddot = mu_red * (a^2 / 2) * cos(2*omega_orb*t) * (e_+^ij and e_x^ij)

  T^{TT}_ab = - (1/2) Q_ddot_ab^TT
