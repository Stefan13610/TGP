[Test 5] 2PN Shapiro time delay (Cassini-class)
==============================================================================

Standard Cassini gives gamma-1 < 2.3e-5 (1PN). 2PN Shapiro delay
requires deeper modelling. From hyperbolic g_tt expansion:

  g_tt^TGP/(-c^2) = 1 - 2U + 2U^2 - (7/3)U^3 + ...
  g_tt^GR/(-c^2)  = 1 - 2U + 2U^2 - (3/2)U^3 + ...

The 1PN and Newtonian terms (-2U, +2U^2) match EXACTLY -> Cassini
gamma=1 satisfied. Difference at U^3 (2PN level).

  U at solar grazing photon: 2.122e-06
  Estimated 2PN time delay shift: |Delta t| ~ (5/6) * U^3 * R/c = 3.985e-15 s
  Cassini timing precision (2002 conjunction): ~ 10^-9 s
  Bound: |Delta t|_2PN < 1e-09 s

  Verdict: |Delta t|_2PN ~ 4.0e-15 s << 1e-9 s -> PASS
