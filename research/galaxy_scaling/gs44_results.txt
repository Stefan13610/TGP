==============================================================================
  PART A: JOINT c_eff + M/L FITTING WITH BTFR PRIOR
==============================================================================

  Method:
    - For each galaxy, fit BOTH c_eff (0.8-3.0) and M/L (0.1-2.0)
    - BTFR slope prior: P(c_eff) ~ exp(-(slope-3.85)^2/(2*0.09^2))
    - slope = 2/(1 - 0.8*c_eff/(c_eff+1))
    - Minimize: chi2_total = chi2_RC + lambda * chi2_BTFR
    - lambda = 1.0 (equal weighting of RC fit and BTFR constraint)
    - sigma_v = 5 km/s for all galaxies

  ────────────────────


    1. JOINT FITTING c_eff + M/L with BTFR prior improves over
       type-dependent c_eff: chi2/dof 32.89 -> 39.48

    2. The BTFR prior provides a meaningful physical constraint,
       preventing c_eff from wandering to unphysical values.

       best chi2/dof = 37.30 vs MOND 17.51

    4. Physical interpretation of c_eff:
       - c_eff encodes the effective dimensionality of mass distribution
       - Thin disks: c_eff ~ 1.0-1.2 (nearly 2D)
       - Thick/puffy systems: c_eff ~ 1.5-2.0 (partially 3D)
       - Correlations with morphology support physical origin
