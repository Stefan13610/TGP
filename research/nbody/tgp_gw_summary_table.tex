\medskip
\noindent\textbf{Key results:}
\begin{enumerate}[nosep]
  \item $c_{\rm GW} = c_0$ is an \textbf{algebraic identity} in TGP:
        gravitational and electromagnetic waves propagate through the same
        substrate $\Gamma$, so their phase velocities are equal by construction.
        This is not a fine-tuned parameter but a structural consequence.
  \item The scalar breathing mode has mass $m_{\rm sp} = \sqrt{\gamma}$
        of order $H_0 \sim 10^{-18}$\,Hz, placing its cutoff frequency
        far below the LIGO band ($\sim 10$--$10^3$\,Hz).
        Non-detection in GW170817 is therefore \textbf{expected}.
  \item The breathing mode is detectable in the PTA band ($\sim$\,nHz)
        with amplitude $\sim 10^{-17}$, distinguishable from
        the stochastic GW background by its monopolar (isotropic)
        angular pattern.
\end{enumerate}
